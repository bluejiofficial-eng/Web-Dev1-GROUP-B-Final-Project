Become a Member of a Cooperative That Grows With You

Join TerraLink Cooperative and gain access to inclusive financial services, shared ownership benefits, and a trusted community committed to long-term progress.

Apply for Membership View Benefits

Membership Benefits

Enjoy practical advantages designed to support your financial stability and everyday needs.

💳

Priority Access to Services

Members receive first access to cooperative loan programs, savings products, and support services.

📈

Dividend Participation

Qualified members share in annual cooperative earnings through patronage refunds and dividends.

🛒

Exclusive CoopMart Offers

Get member-only discounts on selected products and services from TerraLink CoopMart partners.

Eligibility and Requirements

Applicants should meet the cooperative's standard membership qualifications before submission.

✅

Basic Qualification

Must be at least 18 years old and willing to comply with TerraLink Cooperative policies and bylaws.

🪪

Valid Identification

Submit one government-issued ID and supporting personal information for account verification.

📝

Membership Application

Complete the official application form and attend the required membership orientation session.

💰

Initial Capital Share

Pay the prescribed membership fee and initial share capital contribution upon approval.

Membership Types

Choose the membership option that matches your role and participation level in the cooperative.

Regular Member

Full Voting and Dividend Privileges

Regular members maintain active share capital and are eligible for voting rights, loans, and annual patronage benefits.

Associate Member

Access to Core Cooperative Services

Associate members may access selected products and programs while completing requirements for regular membership conversion.

Institutional Member

Partnership-Based Cooperative Enrollment

Organizations and groups can enroll through institutional arrangements subject to cooperative screening and board approval.
