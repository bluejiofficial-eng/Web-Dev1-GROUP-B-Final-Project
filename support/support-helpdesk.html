Support Center

How can we help you?

Find answers to common questions about membership, loans, and services.

👥

How do I become a member?

Complete the membership application form, submit one valid ID, and attend the orientation to finalize your enrollment.

📄

What documents are needed for a loan?

Prepare a completed loan form, valid IDs, proof of income, and any required supporting documents based on the loan type.

⏳

How long does loan approval take?

Most applications are reviewed within 3 to 7 business days after all required documents are submitted.

💵

What is the minimum share capital?

The minimum initial share capital starts at PHP 1,000, subject to the cooperative's latest membership guidelines.

🖥️

Can I apply for a loan online?

Yes. You can use our Online Application page to submit your request and receive updates from our support team.

📈

What are the loan interest rates?

Interest rates vary by product. Visit the Loans page to compare current regular and special loan offers.

🏦

How do I check my account balance?

You may check your balance through branch assistance, official support channels, or your registered member account access.

🎯

What is the dividend payout schedule?

Dividend distributions are typically released annually after financial review and board approval.

✍️

How do I update my personal information?

Submit an update request at your nearest branch or through official online channels with valid supporting documents.

Our team is ready to assist you.
